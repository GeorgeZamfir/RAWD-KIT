We are only as abled as the web environment in front of us!

good wally is a web accessibility consultancy based in Toronto lead by George Zamfir.

We can help you with accessibility evaluations and strategy, technical solutions, WCAG 2 & AODA compliance, training and speaking at your event or project kick-off; — bottom line:

We can create abled web environments; and in the process, empower your team

Ultimately the goal for web accessibility as an industry is to make ourselves obsolete. What does that mean? Web accessibility should not be a specialization but rather just another skill all web professionals have; and that's when accessibility will be business as usual.
